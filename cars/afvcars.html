Eco friendly does not have to be purely functional and slow, it can also be beautiful and fast. Here are some of the best eco friendly cars.
Hybrid

Hybrid cars make use of an electric motor alongside its internal combustion engine. They usually have batteries to power the electric motor and make use of braking or the engine to recharge the batteries of the electric motor.

Ferrari LaFerrarimore_vert

Ferrari

Ferrari LaFerrariclose

Classified more as a "mild hybrid", rather than a hybrid, the La Ferrari is the first hybrid from the famed Italian automaker. Unlike a regular hybrid, it does not have a battery and thus is not capable of electric only operation. The KERS system, alongside the 6.3L V12, produces a combined power output of 789 HP.

Porsche 918more_vert

Porsche

Porsche 918close

The Porsche 918 is a plug-in hybrid hypercar, touted by Top Gear's Richard Hammond as the future, because of its electric only range, everyday usability and high level of performance. The 4.6L V8 and electric motors at the front and rear have a combined output of 887 HP, which is delivered to all 4 wheels.

McLaren P1more_vert

McLaren

McLaren P1close

The P1, is McLaren's plug-in hybrid hypercar and successor to its legendary F1 of the 90's. Being part of the hypercar "trinity", the P1 is equipped with a twin turbo 3.8L V8 and an electic motor, which delivers a combined output of 904 HP to its rear wheels.

BMW i8more_vert

BMW

BMW i8close

The BMW i8 is Bavaria's plug-in sports hybrid. It was not made to be the fastest car on the planet but rather one of the most eco friendly sports cars. But eco friendly does not mean slow, its turbocharged 1.5L straight 3 engine is helped by 2 electric motors both at the front and the rear produce a combined output of 357 HP

Honda CR-Zmore_vert

Honda

Honda CR-Zclose

Contrary to what most people think, petrolheads know that power is not everything, and this is certainly true for the CR-Z. The CR-Z is the successor to the legendary CR-X of the 80's and 90's. Although not as fast as its predecessor, it is definitely more eco friendly to help protect mother nature. It is powered by a 1.5L straight 4 and an electric motor, which produces a combined power figure of 130 HP. It is sent to the front wheels via a 6 speed manual or a CVT.

Electric

Electric vehicles replace internal combustion engines with electric motors and fuel tanks with batteries. They can be recharged using recharging stations or through regenerative braking.

Tesla Roadsterclose

Essentially an electric Lotus Elise, the Tesla Roadster is one of the first electric only sports cars available to the public. Instead of finding a Toyota powerplant, the Roadster is powered by a 3-phase, 4-pole induction electric motor, producing a healthy 248 HP, fed through a BorgWarner single speed, fixed transmission.
