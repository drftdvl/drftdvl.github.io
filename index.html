Moving away from oil

As global warming gets worse, machines in general are being made to be more efficient while being more environmentally friendly.
Car emissions and the dwindling supply of oil are a growing concern, and automakers have been scrambling to improve their techonology rapidly to make alternative fuel vehicles a practical choice.
